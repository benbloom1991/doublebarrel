"Burnout" is a poetic and empowering portrait of Aotearoa's infamous boy racers; an unapologetic and misunderstood counter culture. The film focuses on the idea of escape through the unexpected, following a small town kid and the freedom that comes with his car.

A film about a kid and a car
set in Rāhui Pōkeka, Aotearoa
